Gem Knight

Congratulations on being selected by the World Gem, you need to compete with three other teams and find world gem with your partners. The winners will receive a generous reward and the player who put the gem back will be awarded the title of gem knight. There is only one gem, and only one team will win. The failed players can only stay in this cursed world forever! Are you ready? Find the gem to become the final winner now!
