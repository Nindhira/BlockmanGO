Jailbreak

In this city you can play as police or prisoner.

When you are a cop: Don't attack PRISONERs or you'll be thrown in the prison. There are Wanted Criminals at large, trace and arrest them for rewards.

When you are a prisoner: Collect Books for Keys or Shovels, you can use them to break out of the jail. Then rob everywhere as you want!
