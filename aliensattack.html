Aliens Attack

A large crowd of aliens are going to attack the earth. You and your companions need to fight for the base! When player is in the alien's field of vision, aliens will preferentially attack the player. Otherwise, they will attack the base first. Game over if the base is completely destroyed! Defeating aliens can earn monetary rewards to buy weapons and equipment in shop.
